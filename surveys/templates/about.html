About SurveyMaker

SurveyMaker is a powerful and user-friendly web application designed to help individuals and organizations create, manage, and analyze surveys with ease.

Why SurveyMaker?

Whether you're gathering feedback, conducting research, or running polls, SurveyMaker provides a seamless experience with intuitive tools and real-time results. Built with Django, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL, and JWT authentication, it ensures both performance and security.

Key Features

  • Create customized surveys with multiple question types
  • Secure login and token-based authentication
  • Real-time survey analytics
  • Mobile-friendly and responsive design
  • Simple and clean dashboard for survey management

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower everyone—from students to professionals—to collect meaningful data and make informed decisions through easy-to-use survey tools.
